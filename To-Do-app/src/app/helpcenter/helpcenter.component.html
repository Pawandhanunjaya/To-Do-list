What is TO-DO?

ToDo is a digital notebook application that blends notes, tasks and a daily planner into one streamlined workflow. It's simple to learn, but powerful enough to replace numerous tools that you might have previously used.

Sophisticated Note-taking​

To-Do is built on a powerful block-style text which allows you to take notes in ways you never could before.
Write and embed countless content types in either a traditional document or on an infinite canvas. / inserter, dynamic page linking with backlinks, code blocks with syntax highlighting, and much more.

Get things done with Tasks​

Ideas are nothing until you make them real. To-Do has built-in task management capabilities.
Tasks can be easily created anywhere within your workspace. They can be tracked per page or aggregated to your Daily Notes to make sure you don't lose track of anything. Use color-coding to prioritize them or cancel those that you no longer need.

Learn More »

Plan your day with Daily Notes​

Daily Notes are dynamic documents created for each calendar day.
Use them to log journal entries, take notes throughout the day, or to plan out your day's tasks. Any tasks you don't complete will roll over to the next day so you don't lose track of anything.
